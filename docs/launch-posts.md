## Product Hunt

**Tagline:** Open-source feedback operations for teams that want customer insight they can trust.

**Description:** Nexcus helps teams build surveys, publish link and SDK experiences, collect real customer responses, inspect response analytics, export safely, and connect downstream workflows. It is designed around tenant isolation, server-side credentials, PostgreSQL persistence, Redis/Valkey support, and an open-source deployment path. Nexcus is a clean-room Formbricks-style implementation and does not ship enterprise-only upstream modules.

**Known limit for launch:** hosted Render infrastructure, email verification/password reset, billing, and provider-specific outbound integrations require account-level configuration before they are advertised as generally available.
